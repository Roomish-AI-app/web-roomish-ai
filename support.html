Support

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Roomish AI?

Roomish AI is an app for photorealistic interior transformations powered by AI.

It allows you to change the style, color palette, furniture, and overall atmosphere of your room — without renovation.

You upload a photo, and Roomish AI shows how your actual room could look, generating realistic design results rather than abstract concepts.

More advanced modules for interior editing and photorealistic transformations will be added in future updates.

2. Which devices are supported?

Roomish AI currently works on iPhone with iOS 17 and above.

An Android version is coming soon.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No — the app works completely anonymously.

  • No sign-up required
  • No email or password needed
  • Just download and start using

4. What is included in the free version?

  • 3 free generations (usable in any module)
  • Access to all core features
  • Ability to save results to "My Rooms"

5. What does the subscription include?

  • Unlimited generations
  • No watermarks
  • Access to all features
  • Future premium features as they're released

6. Will my subscription auto-renew?

Yes, unless you turn off auto-renewal in your App Store settings.

7. How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime in your Apple ID settings.

After cancellation, you keep full access until the end of the current billing period.

8. Do you store uploaded photos?

Yes. Photos and generated results are stored in "My Rooms".

  • Photos remain until you delete them manually
  • You can delete any photo anytime from the app
  • If you're inactive for 90 days without a subscription, stored photos are automatically deleted
  • Deleted photos are permanently removed from our servers

We never use your photos to train AI models or share them with third parties.

9. Can anyone see the photos I upload?

No. Your images remain private and are processed securely.

10. How do I delete my data?

To delete your photos and generated results:

  • Open "My Rooms" in the app
  • Delete any photos you want removed
  • They are immediately and permanently deleted from our servers

To remove all app data:

  • Delete the Roomish AI app from your device
  • All anonymous identifiers and cached data will be removed
  • If you have no active subscription and don't reinstall within 90 days, all stored photos will be automatically deleted

11. How long does image generation take?

About 30 seconds from uploading your photo to seeing the result.

The actual AI generation takes 10–15 seconds, plus time for uploading and processing.

Time may vary depending on server load and image complexity.

12. Why might the result look inaccurate?

Common reasons include:

  • Poor lighting
  • Strong perspective distortion
  • Hidden corners or blocked areas
  • Overly cluttered photos

For best results, take photos with good lighting and clear views of the room.

13. How can I contact support?

You can reach us via email: support@roomish.ai

We typically respond within 24-48 hours.
