Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 25, 2025

THIS PRIVACY POLICY EXPLAINS HOW ROOMISH AI ("WE," "US," OR "OUR") COLLECTS, USES, AND PROTECTS INFORMATION WHEN YOU USE THE ROOMISH AI MOBILE APPLICATION (THE "APP" OR THE "SERVICE").

BY USING THE APP, YOU AGREE TO THE PRACTICES DESCRIBED IN THIS PRIVACY POLICY.

IF YOU DO NOT AGREE, DO NOT USE THE APP.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or biometric identifiers.

We collect only the minimum data required to operate the App and deliver AI-based functionality.

1.1. User Content

  • Photos you upload
  • Reference images
  • Generated results

1.2. Technical Data (Non-Personal)

Collected automatically:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version
  • Anonymous technical identifiers (Firebase Installation ID)
  • Crash logs and performance metrics
  • Basic usage analytics

These identifiers:

  • cannot identify you as an individual
  • are deleted when you uninstall the App

1.3. Subscription Data

When purchasing a subscription, we receive:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
  • Anonymous transaction identifiers (from App Store)

We never receive or store your credit card details.

2. No User Accounts

The App operates without user accounts or sign-in.

  • You do not need to create an account to use the App
  • We do not collect email addresses, names, or login credentials
  • All users are anonymous

3. How We Use the Data

Data is used solely to:

  • operate the App and its AI features
  • generate interior designs and stylized images
  • store photos and results in "My Rooms"
  • maintain performance and fix crashes
  • enable subscriptions and restore purchases
  • support users who contact us

We do not use your content for AI training or dataset creation.

4. Storage and Deletion of Photos and Files

4.1. Photo Storage

Uploaded photos and generated results are stored on our servers to enable the Service.

  • Photos are stored in "My Rooms" and remain until you delete them
  • When you delete a photo from the App, it is permanently removed from our servers
  • We do not keep backups of deleted files

4.2. Automatic Deletion After Inactivity

If you do not have an active subscription and remain inactive for 90 days:

  • All stored photos and generated results are automatically deleted
  • This helps us manage server resources efficiently

If you have an active subscription:

  • Your photos and results are retained as long as your subscription is active
  • After subscription cancellation, the 90-day inactivity rule applies

We recommend saving important photos and designs to your device before canceling your subscription or if you plan to be inactive.

4.3. Immediate Deletion by the User

When you delete a file inside the App:

  • it is removed immediately from our servers
  • we do not keep backups
  • deleted content cannot be restored

5. Subscriptions and Payment Processing

All subscription purchases are processed through Apple App Store.

We receive only:

  • Subscription status (active, expired, trial)
  • Anonymous transaction identifiers

We never receive:

  • Your credit card details
  • Your Apple ID email
  • Your billing address
  • Any payment information

Subscription management (cancellation, refunds) is handled entirely by Apple.

New Users:

  • Each new user receives 3 free image generations
  • Free generations are granted once per device

PRO Subscriptions:

  • Weekly, monthly, and annual subscriptions are available
  • PRO subscriptions provide unlimited image generations
  • Subscriptions do not unlock extra features â€” they remove generation limits only

Free Trial:

  • A 3-day free trial may be available depending on App Store eligibility
  • If not cancelled at least 24 hours before the trial ends, it converts to a paid subscription

Auto-Renewal:

  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the billing period

6. Age Requirements

Roomish AI does not contain harmful or sensitive content.

The App is suitable for users aged 4+ in accordance with App Store guidelines.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data.

We only share minimal required data with service providers that help operate the App, such as:

  • cloud hosting providers
  • analytics services
  • performance and crash reporting tools

These providers cannot use your data for their own purposes.

We may disclose limited information only if required by law.

8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards:

  • encrypted data transmission (TLS/HTTPS)
  • restricted access to servers
  • automatic deletion processes
  • routine monitoring for anomalies

No system is fully immune to risks, but we take reasonable measures to protect stored data.

9. User Rights

Users may:

  • delete photos or generated results at any time
  • delete the App to remove all data and anonymous identifiers
  • contact support for assistance

Since we do not collect or store personal data (no accounts, no emails, no names), certain data protection rights (such as data correction or export) do not apply.

If you delete the App from your device:

  • all anonymous identifiers are removed
  • stored photos in "My Rooms" will be deleted after 90 days of inactivity (if no subscription)

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

The "Effective Date" at the top will reflect the latest revision.

Continued use of the App after changes means acceptance of the updated Policy.

11. Contact
